Privacy Policy

Great Physician App
Effective Date: March 13, 2026  ·  Updated: March 24, 2026

Section 1

Introduction

The Kiln Ministries, a nonprofit organization registered in Tennessee, operates the Great Physician app (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

The Great Physician app provides Scripture-based biblical counseling through four AI companions — Barnabas, Ethan, Anna, and Lydia — as well as an Inductive Bible Study feature for group use. We take the privacy and sensitivity of your personal and spiritual information seriously.

Section 2

Information We Collect

2a. Counseling Conversations

Your counseling conversations are not stored. They exist only in memory during your session and are permanently discarded when you close the app or start a new session.

When you use the counseling or Go Deeper features of the App, your messages are processed in real time to generate a response. Conversation content is not stored in any database, is not logged, and is not retained after your session ends. No one at The Kiln Ministries can read your conversations.

2b. Session Identifiers

The App assigns an anonymous session ID to each counseling session to maintain continuity during that session only. This identifier does not identify you personally and is discarded when the session ends. We do not require you to create an account or provide your name or email address to use the counseling features.

2c. Inductive Bible Study Feature

If you use the Inductive Bible Study feature, you will be asked to provide:

  • Your name — to identify you to other members of your study group
  • Your email address (optional) — for potential study-related notifications

This information is stored in our secure database solely to facilitate the shared study group experience. Your name will be visible to other participants in the same study. Your email address, if provided, will not be shared publicly and will only be used for study-related communication.

2d. Device and Usage Data

We may collect standard technical data such as device type, operating system version, and app usage patterns. This data is used solely for maintaining and improving the App and is not linked to any personal information.

Section 3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide the biblical counseling and Bible study experience within the App
  • Maintain and improve the App's performance and reliability
  • Facilitate group Bible study participation
  • Comply with legal obligations if required by law

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Section 4

Counseling Privacy

Your counseling conversations are private. Because conversations are not stored, The Kiln Ministries staff cannot access, review, or monitor your counseling sessions. This is by design — we believe your spiritual conversations should remain between you, God, and the AI companion you have chosen.

Please be aware that this App does not provide the legal protections of licensed professional counseling (such as therapist-client privilege). If you require legally protected counseling, please seek a licensed professional counselor or pastor.

Section 5

Crisis Situations

If you express thoughts of harming yourself or others, the App will direct you to emergency resources. Because conversations are not stored, no session data is transmitted to ministry staff. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services (911) or the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline immediately.

Section 6

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — powers the AI counseling responses
  • OpenAI — used for document embedding in our knowledge base
  • Supabase — secure database storage for Inductive Bible Study data
  • Railway — cloud hosting infrastructure

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them. We have selected these services based on their security standards and do not authorize them to use your data for their own purposes beyond what is necessary to operate the App.

Section 7

Data Retention

Counseling conversation data is not retained — it exists only in memory during your session and is permanently discarded when the session ends.

Inductive Bible Study data (name, optional email, and study responses) is retained for as long as the study exists. You may request deletion of your study data by contacting us at the address below. We will honor deletion requests within 30 days.

Section 8

Children's Privacy

The Great Physician app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Section 9

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted database storage and restricted access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Section 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
