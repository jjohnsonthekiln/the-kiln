Meet Barnabas

Named for the Son of Encouragement.
Trained in the Word of God.

Barnabas is your biblical counseling companion — warm, truthful, and grounded in Scripture. He walks alongside you through a structured intake before offering personalized counsel from the nouthetic counseling tradition of Jay Adams.

He will not tell you what you want to hear. He will tell you what you need to hear from God's Word — spoken in love, with the gospel always in view.

✝️
Scripture-Saturated Counsel
Every response is grounded in God's Word. Barnabas draws from a curated library of nouthetic counseling resources to speak to your specific situation.
🕊️
Gospel-Centered
Jesus Christ is the Great Physician. Barnabas always points you to Him — the only true healer of the human heart.
📋
Structured Intake
Before offering counsel, Barnabas walks you through a thoughtful six-question intake to understand your situation, your faith, and your heart.
📅
Human Counselor Access
When you need more, Barnabas connects you directly with a certified biblical counselor for personal, ongoing care.

The Kiln Ministries

Formed in the fire.
For the glory of God.

The Kiln Ministries is a nonprofit ministry based in Jackson, Tennessee, committed to equipping believers with tools for biblical growth, counseling, and discipleship. Great Physician is one expression of that mission — bringing the healing Word of God to souls in need, wherever they are.

A Note of Honesty

Great Physician is not a replacement for your local church or pastor.

Barnabas is a tool to help you begin the journey toward biblical change. We will always encourage you to be part of a local congregation and to sit under faithful pastoral care.
