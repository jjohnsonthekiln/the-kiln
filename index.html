Who We Are

Shaped in
the fire

The Kiln Ministries is a biblical counseling ministry committed to walking alongside men, women, and families in the most difficult seasons of life. We believe that Scripture alone speaks with authority and grace into every dimension of human struggle.

Our name reflects a core conviction: that God is the Master Potter, and that suffering, far from being senseless, is often the very furnace in which He does His most transformative work. We come alongside those in the heat of that furnace with the Word, prayer, and the community of the Church.

We are a team of biblical counselors, pastors, and ministry workers serving individuals and families locally and remotely, committed to the local church as the primary context for soul care.

Scripture-Centered

The Bible is sufficient, authoritative, and sufficient for all matters of life and godliness. It is the bedrock of every conversation.

Christ-Exalting

Our goal is never mere behavior change — it is conformity to Christ. We point every hurting soul to the living Savior.

Church-Grounded

We see the local church as God's primary instrument of soul care, and we work in partnership with pastors and elders.

Compassion-Driven

Truth without tenderness is cold. We hold the Word with open hands and meet every person with the mercy we ourselves have received.
