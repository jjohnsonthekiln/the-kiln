Who We Are

A ministry dedicated to helping others know Christ and make Him known.

The Kiln is a ministry committed to two things: biblical counseling and building tools that help people deepen their walk with Christ and share the gospel to a lost and dying world. We believe God uses the fire of trial to shape His people — and that the Church is most effective when it is equipped to care for souls.

What We Do

We offer biblical counseling rooted in the sufficiency of Scripture and the nouthetic tradition — the conviction that God's Word is fully adequate to address every problem of the human heart.

We build mobile applications that bring the healing power of the gospel to anyone, anywhere — free of charge, because the Word of God should never sit behind a paywall.

Everything we do flows from one conviction: that Jesus Christ is the answer to every human need, and that His Church is the instrument through which He makes that known.
